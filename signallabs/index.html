FAQ

Quick answers.

The short version: we work around the tools you already use, keep risky decisions in human hands, and make the first paid step measurable.

Do we have to replace the software we already use?

Usually not. We start with the systems already carrying the work and look for practical ways to connect, operate, or simplify them.

What happens during the $999 audit?

We map the repeated workflow, estimate the time being lost, and deliver a prioritized plan with three to seven specific tools or automations where appropriate.

Will AI be allowed to send things or change records on its own?

Only where you approve it and the risk is low. Sensitive actions can remain drafts, review items, or owner-approved steps.

Are you only working with gyms and studios?

No. That is the strongest starting lane because of firsthand experience, but the same approach applies to service businesses with recurring admin work.

Is the website really included?

Yes. When a straightforward website is needed to support the system we are implementing, the build can be included with paid concierge implementation at no extra build fee. You still own and pay for your domain, hosting, payment processing, or other outside services.

Who owns the automations and what happens if we stop working together?

The ownership and handoff are defined before implementation begins. Whenever the platform allows it, the business owns its accounts, data, documentation, and production workflows.

How quickly can we start?

The free assessment can happen as soon as a time is available. Paid audit clients receive scheduling outreach within 24 hours of payment. Implementation begins after you approve the plan.
